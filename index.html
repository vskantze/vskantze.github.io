About me

I’m a data scientist and researcher specializing in mathematical modelling for personalized nutrition and health. My work bridges advanced statistical methods, machine learning, and integrative biology to turn complex data into actionable health insights. I hold a PhD focused on precision nutrition, and I’m passionate about translating science into real-world solutions.
