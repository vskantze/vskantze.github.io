About me

I’m a data scientist and researcher specializing in mathematical modelling for personalized nutrition and health. My work bridges advanced statistics, machine learning, and integrative omics to derive actionable health insights. I hold a PhD focused on dynamic and integrative modelling for precision nutrition, and am passionate about translating science into real-world positive impact.
